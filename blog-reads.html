Finding The Animal in Business - A for Ant

Why are some businesses described as Unicorns?

For an entrepreneur; a Unicorn status is a symbol that is equivalent to a ‘Ballon d’or’ ; an award presented by some of the most influential groups of investors to a company they believe has a billion dollars worth of potential in the market. This award is not easy to achieve, and maybe a Unicorn isn't your business animal.

It is an overstated fact indeed much of biology & anthropology proves this true, the inexplicable link between humans and animals. This is the very same reason the business world is often likened to the "jungle" - to illustrate an environment where everything goes and certain rules apply. We can pull our PETA signs all day trying to make sense of what should not be allowed in the animal kingdom, or we can use that knowledge to build one of those business animals that thrive in the human kingdom.
